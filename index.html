About

Experienced software engineer and undergraduate researcher with a focus on neutrino detector technologies and data acquisition systems, currently contributing to the ANNIE project at Fermilab by enhancing data quality monitoring systems and maintaining the ToolAnalysis framework. At Erciyes Neutrino Research Group (ENRG), actively developing ENRGDAQ for real-time data storage and visualization, ENRG DocDB for scientific document management, and the ENRG Neutrino Atlas for global experiment mapping. Contributing to TÜBİTAK and ERÜ BAP projects by designing modular data acquisition systems for particle detectors. As a Contract Software Engineer at Kalite Bilgisayar, delivered tailored solutions including a solar panel management system and a cryptocurrency trading automation platform, showcasing expertise in Python, Flask, and Svelte. Proven ability to bridge research and industry with robust, scalable software solutions.
